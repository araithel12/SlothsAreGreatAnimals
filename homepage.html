Endangered Species

Did you know that sloths are an endangered species? There are only 1,500 left in the world. With deforestation, hunting, and destruction to their habitat it's hard for them to survive.

Why They Matter

Sloths matter beacuse they are an integral part of tropical rain forest ecosystems. Deforestation is causing sloths to lose their shelter and food source. They also can't defend themselves from predators when they come to the forest floor. There needs to be a solution to help keep them safe.
