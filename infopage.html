Lets Talk More about Sloths

Sloths are seen as slow animals that sleep all day and dont do much but did you know that slothas actually have a lot of cool facts about them.

  1. Sloths are actually three times stronger than us.
  2. They are faster in water than they are on land.
  3. They can fall 100 feet without injury or getting hurt.
  4. They have a natural camouflage from the algae that grows on their skin.
  5. Sloths spend 90% of their lives hanging upside down.

Sloths are really amazing animals and I think they are often judged because of how they look. I mean look at it, how could you not love them.
