What you want to know about sloths

Other than them being slow moving and the sleepiest animals, sloths have a lot of very interesting facts about them.

Why Sloths are slow

Sloths are slow because they have an extremely low metabolic rate, which means they move very slowly through the trees.

What Sloths Eat

Sloths appetite consist of leaves, twigs, and bugs. Their low metabolic rate means that they can survive off of little to no food. It takes a sloth days to digest their food. They can also eat other things like fruit or bird eggs.
